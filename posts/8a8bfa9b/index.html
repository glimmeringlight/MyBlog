信源与信息熵

马尔可夫信源

信源记忆长度为m+1m+1m+1时，该时刻发出的信号只与之前的mmm个有关系，则称这种信源为马尔可夫信源。对于一阶马尔可夫信源，类似于下面的式子都成立：

p(x1∣x2,x3)=p(x1∣x2)p(x_1\mid x_2,x_3)=p(x_1\mid x_2) p(x1​∣x2​,x3​)=p(x1​∣x2​)

因为x1x_1x1​并不影响x3x_3x3​ 。

离散信源信息熵

定义

离散信源信息熵：

H(X)=E[I(X)]=E[−log⁡p(xi)]=−∑ip(xi)log⁡p(xi)H(X)=E[I(X)]=E[-\log p(x_i)]=-\sum_i p(x_i)\log p(x_i) H(X)=E[I(X)]=E[−logp(xi​)]=−i∑​p(xi​)logp(xi​)

条件信息熵：

H(X∣Y)=∑jp(yj)H(X∣yj)=−∑i,jp(yj)p(xi∣yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)\begin{aligned} H(X\mid Y)=\sum_j p(y_j)H(X\mid y_j)&=-\sum_{i,j}p(y_j)p(x_i\mid y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=-\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j) \end{aligned} H(X∣Y)=j∑​p(yj​)H(X∣yj​)​=−i,j∑​p(yj​)p(xi​∣yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)​

联合信息熵：

H(X,Y)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi,yj)H(X,Y)=-\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i,y_j) H(X,Y)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​,yj​)

重要关系

H(X,Y)=H(Y)+H(X∣Y)H(X, Y)=H(Y)+H(X\mid Y)H(X,Y)=H(Y)+H(X∣Y)

H(X,Y)=H(Y)+H(X∣Y)H(X, Y)=H(Y)+H(X\mid Y) H(X,Y)=H(Y)+H(X∣Y)

证明：

H(Y)+H(X∣Y)=−∑jp(yj)log⁡p(yj)−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(yj)−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(yj)p(xi∣yj)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi,yj)=H(X,Y)\begin{aligned} H(Y)+H(X\mid Y)&=-\sum_j p(y_j)\log p(y_j)-\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=-\sum_{i,j} p(x_i,y_j)\log p(y_j)-\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=-\sum_{i,j} p(x_i,y_j)\log p(y_j)p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=-\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i,y_j)=H(X,Y) \end{aligned} H(Y)+H(X∣Y)​=−j∑​p(yj​)logp(yj​)−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(yj​)−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(yj​)p(xi​∣yj​)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​,yj​)=H(X,Y)​

推广：

H(X1,X2,⋯ ,Xn)=∑inH(Xi∣X1,X2,⋯ ,Xi−1)H(X_1,X_2,\cdots ,X_n)=\sum_i^n H(X_i\mid X_1,X_2,\cdots ,X_{i-1}) H(X1​,X2​,⋯,Xn​)=i∑n​H(Xi​∣X1​,X2​,⋯,Xi−1​)

互信息

定义

I(X;Y)=H(X)−H(X∣Y)I(X;Y)=H(X)-H(X\mid Y) I(X;Y)=H(X)−H(X∣Y)

条件互信息定义

I(X;Y∣Z)=H(X∣Z)−H(X∣Y,Z)=∑i,j,kp(xi,yj,zk)log⁡p(xi∣yj,zk)p(xi∣zk)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y\mid Z)&=H(X\mid Z)-H(X\mid Y,Z)\\ &=\sum_{i,j,k}p(x_i,y_j,z_k)\log \dfrac{p(x_i\mid y_j,z_k)}{p(x_i\mid z_k)} \end{aligned} I(X;Y∣Z)​=H(X∣Z)−H(X∣Y,Z)=i,j,k∑​p(xi​,yj​,zk​)logp(xi​∣zk​)p(xi​∣yj​,zk​)​​

重要关系

I(X;Y)=I(Y;X)I(X;Y)=I(Y;X)I(X;Y)=I(Y;X)

证明：

I(X;Y)=H(X)−H(X∣Y)=−∑ip(xi)log⁡p(xi)+∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)=−∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi)+∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi∣yj)=∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi,yj)p(xi)p(yj)=I(Y;X)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y)&=H(X)-H(X\mid Y)\\ &=-\sum_i p(x_i)\log p(x_i)+\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=-\sum_{i,j} p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i)+\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log p(x_i\mid y_j)\\ &=\sum_{i,j} p(x_i,y_j)\log \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j)}{p(x_i)p(y_j)}=I(Y;X) \end{aligned} I(X;Y)​=H(X)−H(X∣Y)=−i∑​p(xi​)logp(xi​)+i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)=−i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​)+i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​∣yj​)=i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​)p(yj​)p(xi​,yj​)​=I(Y;X)​

0⩽I(X;Y)⩽H(X)0\leqslant I(X;Y)\leqslant H(X)0⩽I(X;Y)⩽H(X)

I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)+I(X;Z∣Y)I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)+I(X;Z\mid Y)I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)+I(X;Z∣Y)

证明：

I(X;Y)+I(X;Z∣Y)=∑i,jp(xi,yj)log⁡p(xi,yj)p(xi)p(yj)+∑i,j,kp(xi,yj,zk)log⁡p(xi∣yj,zk)p(xi∣yj)=∑i,j,klog⁡p(xi,yj)p(xi)p(yj)⋅p(xi,yj,zk)p(yj)p(yj,zk)p(xi,yj)=∑i,j,klog⁡p(xi,yj,zk)p(xi)p(yj,zk)=I(X;Y,Z)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y)+I(X;Z\mid Y)&=\sum_{i,j}p(x_i,y_j)\log \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j)}{p(x_i)p(y_j)}+\sum_{i,j,k}p(x_i,y_j,z_k)\log \dfrac{p(x_i\mid y_j,z_k)}{p(x_i\mid y_j)}\\ &=\sum_{i,j,k}\log \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j)}{p(x_i)p(y_j)}\cdot \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j,z_k)p(y_j)}{p(y_j,z_k)p(x_i,y_j)}\\ &=\sum_{i,j,k}\log \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j,z_k)}{p(x_i)p(y_j,z_k)}=I(X;Y,Z) \end{aligned} I(X;Y)+I(X;Z∣Y)​=i,j∑​p(xi​,yj​)logp(xi​)p(yj​)p(xi​,yj​)​+i,j,k∑​p(xi​,yj​,zk​)logp(xi​∣yj​)p(xi​∣yj​,zk​)​=i,j,k∑​logp(xi​)p(yj​)p(xi​,yj​)​⋅p(yj​,zk​)p(xi​,yj​)p(xi​,yj​,zk​)p(yj​)​=i,j,k∑​logp(xi​)p(yj​,zk​)p(xi​,yj​,zk​)​=I(X;Y,Z)​

I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)I(X;Y,Z)=I(X;Y)若X−Y−ZX-Y-ZX−Y−Z构成马尔科夫链

证明：

I(X;Y,Z)=∑i,j,kp(xi.yj,zk)log⁡p(xi,yj,zk)p(xi)p(yi,zk)=∑i,j,kp(xi.yj,zk)log⁡p(xi∣yj,zk)p(xi)=∑i,j,kp(xi.yj,zk)log⁡p(xi∣yj)p(xi)=I(X;Y)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y,Z)&=\sum_{i,j,k}p(x_i.y_j,z_k)\log \dfrac{p(x_i,y_j,z_k)}{p(x_i)p(y_i,z_k)}\\ &=\sum_{i,j,k}p(x_i.y_j,z_k)\log \dfrac{p(x_i\mid y_j,z_k)}{p(x_i)}\\ &=\sum_{i,j,k}p(x_i.y_j,z_k)\log \dfrac{p(x_i\mid y_j)}{p(x_i)}=I(X;Y) \end{aligned} I(X;Y,Z)​=i,j,k∑​p(xi​.yj​,zk​)logp(xi​)p(yi​,zk​)p(xi​,yj​,zk​)​=i,j,k∑​p(xi​.yj​,zk​)logp(xi​)p(xi​∣yj​,zk​)​=i,j,k∑​p(xi​.yj​,zk​)logp(xi​)p(xi​∣yj​)​=I(X;Y)​

信息传输不等式

对于构成马尔科夫链的随机变量X−Y−ZX-Y-ZX−Y−Z，他们之间的互信息有关系：

I(X;Y)⩾I(X;Z)I(X;Y)\geqslant I(X;Z) I(X;Y)⩾I(X;Z)

证明：

I(X;Y)−I(X;Z)=I(X;Y,Z)−I(X;Z)=I(X;Y∣Z)=∑kp(zk)I(X;Y∣zk)⩾0.\begin{aligned} I(X;Y)-I(X;Z)&=I(X;Y,Z)-I(X;Z)\\ &=I(X;Y\mid Z)\\ &=\sum_k p(z_k)I(X;Y\mid z_k)\geqslant 0. \end{aligned} I(X;Y)−I(X;Z)​=I(X;Y,Z)−I(X;Z)=I(X;Y∣Z)=k∑​p(zk​)I(X;Y∣zk​)⩾0.​
